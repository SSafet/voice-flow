5 · Rules

  • Destination is data, not UI: every capture stores one destination — pasted · kept · → assistant · → session N — and it shows up nowhere on the rows. The filter chips are the only surface of that field; ticket #2’s inbox is the Kept filter, never a fifth tab.
  • Kept items are unread until revisited: tracked silently — the Kept chip count and the Inbox tab count carry it; opening the item clears it. No dots, markers, or action buttons on the rows.
  • Agents nav ≡ pill picker: sessions keep the picker’s numbers, unread rings and ghosts (#14) — gold numbered dots. The assistant is NOT a session: persistent first row, the VoiceFlow pill mark, never a number.
  • A push is ONE flat block, question or not: no cards, no timestamps, no repeated sender names, no edge decorations. The composer attaching to a block IS the ask signal; the answer then attaches beneath it (↳), in order. Otherwise a single composer sits at the bottom of the thread.
  • The panel is secondary: ⌃⌥1–9 on the pill remains the primary way to see and answer agent notifications; this tab is the browsable archive of the same stacks, and nothing here changes the pill flow.
  • Speech is a verb, not a place: the drawer + per-row 🔊 replace the tab. Audio still never auto-plays.
  • Sync boundary (#7): Inbox = captures up, Agents = messages down — the two streams a future mobile client syncs, unchanged.
